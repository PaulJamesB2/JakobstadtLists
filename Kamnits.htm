Parish Book 13 - Pages 183a through 183b
Kamnits Jewish House Lists - 1797

 


On the farm and in the inns live Jews:


On the farm :
The wine distiller Berek Simanowitz, age 54
    His wife Rafka, 32
    His son Jankel, 7
    His son Jossel, 5
    His son Salmann, 3
    His daughter Rivka, 14

INNS:

In the estate's Janiske Inn :
The Jewish Innkeeper Michel Minlowitz, age 30
    His wife Feige, 28
    His son Salmann, 15
    His son Mausche, 9
    His son David, 3
    His daughter Sorre, 1

In the estate's Philipovka Inn :
The Jewish Innkeeper Aron Michelowitz, age 31
    His wife Bliema, 23
    His son Isaac, 10
    His son Levin, 2
   
    The maid Rivke, 12

In the estate's Patnitz Inn :
The Jewish Innkeeper Hirsch Hirschowitz, age 40
    His wife Sheine, 20
    His son Leibe, 1
    His daughter Chane, 6

Tenent Itzik Lipmanowitz, age 24          [Lipmann]
    His wife. Ester, 20

KAMNITZ
The 30th of April, 1797
