In the Pallei Inn on the Duna River:
The Jewish Innkeeper Chaim Mauschel, age 24 [son of Mauschel Chaim at Sosse Inn]
      His wife Ette, 24
      His son Gerson, 4

The Jewish butcher Judel Jossel, from Birsen, Lithuania
He is registered in Birsen, Lithuania, but has taken up residence at the Pallei Inn on the Duna River. As he is already registered on the Census (Seelenlisten) there [in Lithuanian], he is not registered here [in Courland].

      His son Isaac, 15
      His son Jacob, 8
      His son Mischel, 1/2
      His daughter Fromme, 11
      His daughter Rive, 3
      
The Schoolmaster Jankel, from Birsen, Lithuania.
He is registered in Birsen, Lithuania. As he is already registered on the Census (Seelenlisten) there [in Lithuanian], he is not registered here [in Courland].

Jude Isaac, from Sauken in Kurland, brother of the distiller [Meyer Isaac]
He is registered in Sauken and his parents are living there. As he is already registered on the Census (Seelenlisten) there [in Sauken], he is not registered here.
