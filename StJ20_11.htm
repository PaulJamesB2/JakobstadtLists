Notes:
--This census was recorded in 1813. 
--All of the billet receipts were dated 1807, except one for 11-23-1811.
--The 1811 Proof of Tax Payment column provides 1807 Oklad numbers.  The 1807 Oklad no longer exists.
--For the 1816 Tax Register column, the surname or patronymic in all caps indicates the name used for indexing.  If the indexing name differs from the individual's name, the indexing name is including in brackets. (For example:  An 1816 reference for "Elias Jossel [ELIAS]" indicates that "Elias Jossel" can be found under the Patronymic "ELIAS" in the 1816 census.)
--The receipt numbers for the 1816 oklad were penciled in after this Oklad was completed. They are used to reference the 1816 Jakobstadt City Oklad (StJ20b).
--This Oklad lists Men only
--JBO = Jakobstadt Burger Oklad
--Editor's notes are in brackets []
