Podoney Jewish House Lists - 1797

 

On the various farmsteads of the estate and its inns live Jews:

INNS:

In the new inn on the Duna River:
Innkeeper Mausche Mendelowitz, age 25
         His wife Gita, 24
         His son Wulf, 9

Tenent Leibe Jankelowitz, 20
         His wife Chaike, 18
         His son Mendel, 5 weeks

Tenent Peter, a Lithuanian, age 50

     His son Joseph, 6
     His stepson Peter, 16
