WASSERMANN

 

  • StJ19a, JBO Country 1811, in Nerft, Oklad #48-54: 
    --Moses Abraham Wassermann 32; son Isaac 10. 
    --Hirsch Schmuel Wassermann 48; son Israel 16; son Salomon 14; son Isaac 13; son Marcus 10.
  • StJ20b, JBO City 1816, Oklad #115-116, p. 107: Moses Abraham Wassermann 36; wife Chaye; son Isaac 14.
  • 1858 JBO Country, Family 94, Oklad 285, Hirsch Wassermann, son of Isaac, 27. Wife Margolie.

Note: Hirsch and Abraham Wassermans were probably brothers. Probably not related to family of Pinkas Sorroch Wassermann in Par 70 Dannenfeld Private Estate
