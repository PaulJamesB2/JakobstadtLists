Ilsensee Jewish House Lists - 1797

 


In the various farmsteads on the estates and in the inns live Jews:

INNS:

In the estate's Smelin Inn :
Innkeeper Elias Schlomowitz, age 40
    Elias' wife Sorre, 35
    Elias' first son Leiser, 22
          Leiser's wife Dvorke, 25
          Leiser's daughter Nochonke, 2
    Elias' second son Schlome, 11
    Elias' third son Jankel, 8
    Elias' fourth son Nissen, 1
    Elias' first daughter Dverke, 18
    Elias' second daughter Jochvet, 6

    The servant Kastril, 15

Tenent Jankel Leibowitz, age 26
    His wife Beile, 21
    His first daughter Elke, 3
    His second daughter Pesia, 1

In the estate's Osinov Inn :
Innkeeper Levin Judewitz, age 30
    His wife Ester, 25
    His daughter Chave, 1
    His brother Theodor, 20


FARMSTEADS:

In the estate's Zinitrauski Farmstead :
Theodor Leiserowitz, age 25
    His wife Sorre, 40
    His first son Mausche, 11
    His second son Levin, 4
    His third son Abraham, 2
    His daughter Ida, 7
    His mother Ester, 50


The farm's distiller, Juddel Leiserowitz, age 47
    His wife Ester, 36
    His first son Matis, 18
    His second son Meyer, 7
    His third son, Orel, 3
    His first daughter Merka, 14
    His second daughter Dreike, 11
    His third daughter Beile, 9
    His fourth daughter Henke, 5

ILSENSEE
The 30th of April, 1797
