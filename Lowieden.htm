Lowieden Jewish House Lists - 1797

 

On the farm and in the inns live Jews:


On the farm :
The wine distiller, Isaac Markelewitz, age 21          [Rosenberg]
    His wife Rive, 20
    His son Leibe, 4
    His son Marcus, 1

               Jankel's daughter Brentze, 1

    The apprentice Leiser, 14
