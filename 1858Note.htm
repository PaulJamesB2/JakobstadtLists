Notes for the 1858 Tax Registers

These 1858 Tax Registers are partial reconstructions of lost census documents.   Several different documents were used to reconstruct these lost tax registers.  Existing Jakobstadt Military Recruits lists for 1868 and 1871 reference many of the individuals from the 1858 Tax Registers, along with their 1858 Tax (Oklad) Numbers.  Several census documents from 1797 through 1871 were used to determine the family relationships.  Please note the following:

    • References to ealier census documents are included in these reconstructed documents.  
    • It should be noted that some of these men and women may not have been married in 1858.  Some of the marriages may have taken place as late as 1871.   This is because the marriages were mentioned in the later documents.
    • The Military Recruits Lists used for these reconstructions also referenced the 1858 Family Numbers from the 1858 Revision Lists.  These are included in these reconstructed Tax Registers, although they were not on the original documents.
