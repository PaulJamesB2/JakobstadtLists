BERKOWITZ

  • StJ19b, JBO Country, 1816, Oklad No. 24, Family No. 13, pp 38 & 39. Fischke (Feivisch) Berkowitz, 60 (wrong age); daughter Raske, 13; son Hirsch, 20.
  • StJ19b, JBO Country, 1818 additions, Oklad Nos. 197 & 199: Behr-Feivisch Berkowitz, 25; Behr's brother Hosias Feivisch Berkowitz, 16. They are both from Kalkunen. They are noted as omitted from the Oklad, but their father is Oklad No. 24, Fischke Berkowitz.
